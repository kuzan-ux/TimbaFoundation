ABOUT US

TIMBA ENTREPRENEUR FOUNDATION was established in the year 2015, it is a Non Governmental Organisation that reaches out to young people across communities in Nigeria. As an organisation, we provide training around the core of leadership, personal development, business development in our 2 weeks intensive training FREE OF CHARGE.

One of the major problems the African society is faced with is child and youth crime. The root cause of this problem is immediately traced to the presence of limited economic opportunities and lack of capacity development for leadership and innovation. The subject of entrepreneurship development and promotion is quite germane to Africa's economic growth and with inadequate institutional attention being paid to this problem, there is an urgent and fundamental need for private/ corporate efforts to alleviate the problem of child and youth crime and vices in Africa.

Timba Entrepreneur Foundation is designed to bridge such gap by equipping the people in our target population with the knowledge and skills they require to empower them economically and gain financial independence FREE OF CHARGE.

TIMBA

Vision Statement

To raise a generation a new generation of adequately equipped and empowered African youths driving enterprise growth in Africa, starting from our home country Nigeria.

Mission Statement

To empower young people in grass root communities to become entrepreneurs and leaders through our FREE personal development, business development and vocational skills training. In order for them to journey into financial independence and gain social and economic relevance.

GET TRAINED

In our effort to impact and change the status quo of the society, we reachout to young people in various communities and local government area across the nation without prejudice for them to become financial independent and to acquire leadership, business development and vocational skills FREE OF CHARGE. We are committed to changing our society one person at a time, giving them the freedom of choice of vocation. Timba Entrepreneur Foundation facilitates free vocational trainings across communities to enable participants have easy access to the training. In the training, participants are taught on business development where they take courses on:

  • The art of business
  • Business branding
  • Writing a comprehensive business plan
  • Raising a start up capital
  • Financial intelligence
  • Digital marketing etc.

During the training program, the students are given opportunities to come up with ideas on projects they can do to improve their communities with little or no funds. They independently engage a project(s) from ideation to execution under professional supervision. Our goal is to instill in our beneficiaries the mind set of taking initiatives. That way, they are able to harness and express their leadership capabilities.
